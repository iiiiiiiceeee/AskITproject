How to Reset Your Password

To reset your password for Gmail, follow these steps:

Go to the Gmail Sign-in Page: Open your web browser and go to the Gmail sign-in page (https://mail.google.com/).

Click on "Forgot password?": Underneath the password field, you'll see a link labeled "Forgot password?". Click on it.

Enter Your Email Address: You'll be prompted to enter the email address for which you want to reset the password. Enter your Gmail address and click "Next".

Verification: Google will ask you to verify your identity. Depending on your account settings and security options, you may be asked to enter the last password you remember, answer security questions, or use another verification method such as receiving a verification code via text message or email to a recovery email address. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the verification process.

Reset Your Password: After successfully verifying your identity, you'll be given the option to reset your password. Enter a new password and confirm it. Make sure to choose a strong, secure password.

Completion: Once you've set your new password, you'll receive a confirmation that your password has been changed.

Update Saved Passwords (if necessary): If you've saved your Gmail password in your browser or any password manager, make sure to update it with the new password.

That's it! You've successfully reset your password for Gmail. Make sure to keep your new password secure and consider enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of security on your account.
